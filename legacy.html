Legacy

Sebastien left an example for us to follow: the true extent of his legacy will be defined less in the small gestures documented here, and more on the gestures large and small by which we live our lives. 

Be Kind. Share a Smile.

Tree Planting and Online Guestbook (Book of Condolence)

As part of their service provided to Sebastien's family, Hamlin Hansen Kosloski Funeral Home helped create an online Book of Condolence as well as a way to buy newly-planted trees in his memory. You may, of course, plant trees of your own by which to remember him!
